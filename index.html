Best car tyres

Driving a car is not a privilege but a must for millions of people. And it is worth considering what spare parts to choose for your vehicle as the comfort depends on each detail and especially on tires. There is no need to explain the importance of regular check-out for your auto's tires and the necessity to change them before the winter or summer seasons. Every responsible car owner knows that.  Yet knowing what tire brands are worth your attention could come in handy. That's why we have prepared our rate of the best tires for a car to choosing in 2021.

In our top-7, we have focused on several features that make a certain tire brand the best. That rate is intended for both passengers and crossovers. What makes a good tire for a car the best? Of course, it is its correspondence to the following questions you should answer beforehand.

  1. In what price segment are you planning to buy tires. In this article, we make ratings depending on the price segment to which the tire belongs?
  2. What kind of road surface you will drive most often. It will be mostly asphalt or it will be asphalt and dirt road or only dirt road? For example, tires for offroad driving differ much from city tires.
  3. What is your driving style? Whether you drive carefully or you like driving fast and furious?
  4. Do you prefer quiet or noisy tires as there are appreciators for both these types.

Besides these questions, we have taken into account the diversity of models represented under the same brand, experts' reviews on each brand, and specific features of each brand that distinguish it from others.

To make sure our review will be useful, we can claim our rate is based only on official information provided by each brand we've chosen and on experts opinions we have collected for you. So let's start with our rate right now.

1. Goodyear

No less popular tire brand is Goodyear. It also enters numerous rates of the best tires for a car. In terms of the volume of tires produced, the manufacturer is in first place in North America and second in Europe. Through continuous product improvement and innovation in the manufacturing process, Goodyear has been able to create tires that are flawless in many ways. Just a fact that Apollo lunar rover was equipped with Goodyear's tires.The most popular GoodYear summer models: Assurance Fuel Max, Cargo G, Duragrip, Eagle Authority, Eagle F1 A / S-C, Eagle F1 Asymmetric, EfficientGrip. Eagle F1 summer tires have won numerous professional tests many times. GoodYear winter models are no less in demand: Cargo Ultra Grip, Eagle GT + 4, Fortera HL, FuelMax, Ice Navi, Nordic. The standard tire for professionals and racers is the UltraGrip range.The Goodyear manufacturer pays maximum attention to environmental friendliness, reliability, product safety. Both Goodyear winter and summer tires are free of hazardous chemicals and are economical, durable, and quiet.The special tread pattern provides uniformity of the load, effective drainage of fluid from the tire contact patch. The generously sized side blocks found on many Goodyear models provide stability, handling, and excellent traction and that is the reason to give it the second place among our top tires for a car. ..

2. Bridgestone

This brand needs no special presentation as it is still among the best car tires at various rates and its market share is 20% in the whole tire market. In the middle price segment, it is #1 so we have placed Bridgestone tires on the first place. Its powerful team offers clients the perfect service in a wide variety of countries. Bridgestone quickly became one of the world's largest tire manufacturers and suppliers. The quality of the products is confirmed by the fact that it was the manufacturer Bridgestone who supplied the tires for Formula 1. The tires of this manufacturer are equipped with the best cars in the world including Lexus GS 450h, Ferrari, Maserati, and Audi RS5.This brand offers tires for the winter and summer seasons both. Summer tires (Bridgestone Turanza, Duravis model line, and Escopia) allow the car owner to feel and control his car perfectly, on dry and wet roads. The increased content of silicon in the rubber composition gives a decent grip, sufficiently rigid blocks provide stability, including when cornering. The most popular winter tires by Bridgestone are Blizzak and Ice Cruiser model lines. All of them are installed on a wide range of different types of cars, providing confidence even on difficult, slippery, and snowy trails. Both studded and non-studded Bridgestone winter tires remain soft even in severe frost, thanks to a special tread pattern, they provide fast braking and excellent grip.In general, this brand offers a decent number of tires for a wide range of vehicles with impeccable quality and innovative construction...

3. Apollo

Apollo Tyres is an Indian tire concern, the third-largest in the country, and the most popular in the world due to car best tires' rates. Today, tires are manufactured in India, Africa and Europe. The manufacturer plans to enter the top ten world leaders in the tire industry. The latest Apollo tires have been designed with new technology and are marked 4G. The Apollo Aspire 4G tires have an asymmetrical tread pattern that closely resembles summer Nokian models. The asymmetry of the tread is a clear indicator of the tire's adaptability to high speeds. The shoulder area of the tire is made of strong, strong blocks, and the tread is divided by four longitudinal sipes for water drainage. An intermediate groove is provided for better maneuverability on dry surfaces. The manufacturer promises the new tires stiffness for improved handling and faster steering response. Apollo also says the Aspire 4G tires combine grip and comfort. The rubber compound includes styrene-butadiene and silicon-containing vinyl. So the manufacturer tried to achieve impeccable handling and grip...

Conclusion

When compiling our rating of the best car tires, we were guided not only by the popularity of brands or their range, but also by the realities faced by an Indian driver. This means that brands whose products are intended mainly for European and North American climatic conditions could not take high places in our rating. At the same time, the leadership was taken by the Japanese brand Bridgestone, which is confidently leading on a global scale. We gave second place to Goodyear tires for their excellent performance and high adaptability. But the third place is immediately a lot of brands that can be bought in chain malls or on Amazon - generic tires. They can become a worthy replacement for original equipment, and among them, there are many real bestsellers, besides, the price for them is very democratic.

The fourth and fifth places were shared by local tire manufacturers - Apollo and JK Tires. They are actively developing their production, introducing new technologies, and even if they do not yet keep up with the leaders of the world market, they perfectly keep the high bar. But the last places were taken by not bad manufacturers on a global scale, Firestone and Ceat. But their high price segment and general inadequacy to the demands of Indian motorists brought them to 6th and 7th places, respectively.
