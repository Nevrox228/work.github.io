Saweetie on Her New M.A.C Campaign, Working With Cher and Tips for Being Confident

The rapper’s new Challenge Accepted campaign with M.A.C Cosmetics (featuring Cher!) is all about owning yourself and your craft.
by KARA CUZZONE - JAN 21, 2022

Every item on this page was chosen by an ELLE Canada editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

As a woman in modern society, it can be difficult to find your footing and develop the confidence you need to take on daily challenges—big or small—whether it’s related to work, your personal life, relationships or even wearing makeup.

Launching today, M.A.C Cosmetics’ Challenge Accepted campaign invites customers to use their products to “test and shine a light on their performance.” Who better to emulate this campaign than rapper Saweetie and the icon herself, Cher. Both women never settle and empower their communities to be the best versions of themselves. We chatted with Saweetie about being a part of the campaign, working with Cher and how she stays confident in challenging times.

How would you describe the Challenge Accepted campaign with M.A.C?

We chatted with Saweetie about being a part of the campaign, working with Cher and how she stays confident in challenging times.

I gained confidence from not having confidence. It [has] been a journey for me to know myself. I have to know my weaknesses, my strengths, and I try to strengthen my weaknesses. My confidence comes from expanding my knowledge of myself and the world. I was watching a video that said people who are more confident are more knowledgeable about the world—they [become] knowledgeable of themselves, they know what their purpose is, what their intention is. I feel like the lack of knowledge is like a question mark [within yourself.] Once you alleviate those question marks from within, your confidence goes up.
